Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need previous experience with gardening or farming?

No previous experience is necessary. Our courses are designed to welcome people at all levels, from complete beginners to those with some background in growing.

Where do the courses take place?

Our courses run at various locations across the UK, including working farms, community gardens, and regenerative growing spaces. Specific locations are shared upon booking.

What should I bring to a course?

We'll send you a detailed packing list upon booking, but generally: weather-appropriate clothing, sturdy shoes, a notebook, and an open mind. All tools and materials are provided.

Can you bring courses to our organization or community?

Yes! We offer bespoke workshops and courses for organizations, schools, and communities. Get in touch to discuss your needs and we can create something tailored for your group.

What's included in the course fee?

Course fees include all instruction, materials, tools, and resources. Some courses may include meals - this will be specified in the course description. Accommodation is not typically included unless stated.
