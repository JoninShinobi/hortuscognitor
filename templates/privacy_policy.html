PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: {{ last_updated }}

At Hortus Cognitor, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Data Controller: Hortus Cognitor
Contact Email: hannah@hortuscognitor.co.uk
Website: hortuscognitor.co.uk

We are the data controller responsible for your personal information. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at the email address above.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Subject of enquiry
  • Message content
  • IP address (for security and spam prevention)

2.2 Course Bookings

When you book a course, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Course selection and pricing tier chosen
  • Payment method and payment status
  • Any special requirements or messages you provide

2.3 Payment Information

Payment card details are NOT stored on our servers. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe, our payment processor. We only receive confirmation of successful payments and transaction references. For more information on how Stripe processes your data, please see their privacy policy.

2.4 Cookies and Technical Data

We collect minimal technical data including:

  • Session cookies (essential for website functionality)
  • CSRF tokens (security measure to protect forms)
  • IP address (for security, spam prevention, and rate limiting)

We do NOT use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies. Our website only uses essential cookies required for basic functionality and security.

3. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

Contract Performance

When you book a course, we process your data to fulfill our contract with you - delivering the course you've paid for and communicating important course information.

Legitimate Interests

For contact form enquiries, we process your data based on our legitimate business interests to respond to your queries and potential future course bookings.

Legal Obligation

We retain certain records to comply with UK tax law and accounting requirements.

4. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and confirm your course bookings
  • Send you course confirmation emails and payment receipts
  • Communicate important course updates, dates, and logistics
  • Respond to your contact form enquiries
  • Send payment reminders (for instalment payment plans)
  • Manage our business operations and record-keeping
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting)
  • Prevent fraud and maintain website security

We will NEVER:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Send you marketing emails unless you have explicitly opted in
  • Share your data with third parties except as described in section 5
  • Use your data for purposes other than those stated in this policy

5. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your personal data only with trusted third-party service providers who help us deliver our services:

Stripe (Payment Processing)

We use Stripe to securely process payments. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant and meets the highest security standards for payment processing. View their privacy policy: stripe.com/gb/privacy

SendGrid (Email Delivery)

We use SendGrid to send transactional emails (booking confirmations, payment receipts, course updates). View their privacy policy: twilio.com/legal/privacy

Render (Website Hosting)

Our website and database are hosted on Render's secure servers located in the EU/UK. View their privacy policy: render.com/privacy

All third-party providers are carefully selected and contractually bound to protect your data in accordance with UK GDPR requirements.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

Course Bookings

We retain booking and payment records for 7 years from the date of the course to comply with UK tax and accounting regulations (HMRC requirements).

Contact Form Enquiries

Contact form submissions are retained for 2 years unless you request earlier deletion. This allows us to maintain context for ongoing or potential future enquiries.

Technical Data

IP addresses used for security and rate limiting are retained for 30 days maximum.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

Right of Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data, except where we have a legal obligation to retain it (e.g., tax records).

Right to Restriction of Processing

You can ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

Right to Object

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Where we process data based on consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hannah@hortuscognitor.co.uk
We will respond to your request within one month as required by UK GDPR.

8. How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data:

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption - All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted
  • Secure password storage - Admin passwords are hashed using Django's secure password hashing
  • CSRF protection - All forms are protected against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks
  • Rate limiting - We prevent spam and brute-force attacks through rate limiting
  • Input validation - All user inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent injection attacks
  • PCI-DSS compliance - Payment data is processed by Stripe (we never see your card details)
  • Regular security updates - We keep all software dependencies up to date
  • Access controls - Only authorized personnel can access your personal data

9. International Data Transfers

Our website hosting (Render) stores data on servers located in the EU/UK. Some of our service providers (Stripe, SendGrid) may process data in the USA, but they have appropriate safeguards in place including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and EU-US Data Privacy Framework certification to ensure your data remains protected to UK GDPR standards.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

11. Automated Decision Making

We do not use automated decision making or profiling that has legal or similarly significant effects on you. All booking decisions and communications are reviewed by humans.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If we make significant changes that affect how we use your personal data, we will notify you by email (if you have provided one) or by placing a prominent notice on our website.

13. How to Complain

If you have concerns about how we handle your personal data, please contact us first at hannah@hortuscognitor.co.uk and we will do our best to resolve the issue.
